About Me

Hi, I’m Clara, a passionate game design student and digital artist based in Berlin. With a background in fine art from the National School of Fine Arts in Bulgaria, I focus on creating immersive game concepts and accurately visualizing my ideas.

When I’m not designing games or creating art, you’ll find me exploring the latest trends in the gaming industry or experimenting with new digital and traditional art techniques.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you’d like to collaborate or just say hello!
